Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Enermind Company ("we," "us," "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect data when you use the Enermind Poultry Platform at www.enermindcompany.co.ke.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our platform, we may collect:

  • From sellers: Name, phone number, WhatsApp number, email address, county, farm/business name, listing details.
  • From buyers: Name, phone number (for M-Pesa payment processing).
  • Automatically: Browser type, IP address, pages visited, and other standard web analytics data.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To display your listing on the marketplace and directory
  • To process M-Pesa payments via Pesapal
  • To provide contact details to buyers who pay the unlock fee
  • To send you important updates about your listing (if email provided)
  • To improve the platform and our services

3. Who We Share Your Data With

We share your contact information only in the following circumstances:

  • Pesapal: We share your phone number with Pesapal to process M-Pesa payments. Pesapal is a licensed payment service provider regulated by the Central Bank of Kenya.
  • Buyers: When a buyer pays an unlock fee for your listing, we reveal your phone number, WhatsApp, and location to that buyer only.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by Kenyan law or lawful authority.

We do not sell your personal data to any third parties.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. All payment transactions are handled by Pesapal using industry-standard encryption. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for us to use your data

To exercise these rights, email us at info@enermindcompany.co.ke.

6. Cookies

We use session storage (a browser-based technology) to remember which seller contacts you have unlocked during your current visit. This data is never sent to our servers and is cleared when you close your browser. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our platform is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you by posting a notice on our website.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy questions, email: info@enermindcompany.co.ke
