Farmer Tips

Cut costs, grow profits

💧

Water Management

Ensure clean water at all times. Dehydration increases mortality by up to 15%. Use nipple drinkers to reduce wastage.

📦

Bulk Feed Buying

Buying feed in bulk (10+ bags) typically saves KES 200–400 per bag. Source directly from feed sellers on Enermind.

💉

Vaccination Schedule

Stick to a proper vaccination schedule: Marek's (day 1), Gumboro (day 14), Newcastle (day 21). Prevents costly disease outbreaks.

📊

Record Keeping

Track daily feed intake, mortality, and weight gains. Farmers who keep records earn 20% more on average per cycle.
