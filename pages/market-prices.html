What drives prices?

Price factors to know

🌧️

Season

Bird prices spike during December holidays and Easter. Plan purchases 4–6 weeks early to buy at lower cycle prices.

🚚

Distance

Transport adds KES 50–200 per bird depending on distance. Buying locally saves significant cost on live bird orders.

📦

Quantity

Bulk orders (100+ birds or 10+ feed bags) consistently attract 5–15% discounts from verified Enermind sellers.

✅

Certification

Vaccinated, verified birds command a 10–20% premium — but significantly reduce your mortality risk and vet bills.
